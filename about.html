About Us

Company History

3T Logistics (Pty) Ltd was established in 2022 and has over a has years in the transportation business. No load is too big or too small. Our company transports a variety of general cargo and goods in and around Botswana. From its humble beginnings, the company started out as a home based transport business with the acquisition of a medium sized box truck, initially transporting goods (mostly house hold goods for those moving houses or relocating to other parts of Botswana). We have gradually seen and increase in the loads and we are looking forward to opening a deport in Gaborone to accommodate and efficiently handle the increase in loads. The outsource bigger trucks when ever we get bigger loads to be moved.
