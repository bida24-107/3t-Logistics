Services

3T Logistics (Pty) Ltd is well-established transport company that transport a wide variety of general cargo and goods in and around Botswana.

Cross Border Transport

We offer reliable and efficient trucking solution for businesses moving goods between Botswana and neigboring countries such as South Africa, Namibia, Zambia and others.

Bulk Loads

Bulk loading services ensure the efficient transportation of large quantities of loose materials such as grains, minerals, or aggregates.

Consolidated Loads

Multiple smaller shipments, often from different shippers or multiple loads from the same shipper, are combined into a single full truckload or container.This enables us to offer competitive rates.

Dedicated Contracts

These contracts ensure that dedicated fleet or set of trucks is assigned solery to handle the shipper's freight, offering consinstent capacity, priority service, and customised logistics solutions.

LineHaul

Our line-haul services focus on speed, reliability, cost-effectiveness to ensure timely transit across long routes.

Contract Distribution

Contract distribution ensures reliable, scalable, and customized delivery solutions talilored to client's needs, often supported by advanced technology and performance monitoring.
